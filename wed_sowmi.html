about Us

The Dance & Dance Company is Chennai's very own high specialized and stylized dance company which teaches all western dances in highly international standards in certified and open classes. At Chennai Dance and Dance Academy near greams road, the teaching is at a new level and is also very stimulating as it works on combining energy, elegance and a lot of movement. Our dance company was started in Chennai based on the young passion and dynamic of Mr. Vijay who has learnt and specialized in dancing in the United States and performed in various stage shows in an around Orange County.
